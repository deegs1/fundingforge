About FundingForge

Our mission, vision, and the team behind your grant success

Our Story

FundingForge was built to solve the common challenges nonprofit leaders face: overwhelming workloads, unclear funding strategies, and systems that don't scale. We combine deep grant expertise with strategic thinking to create custom pathways to funding success that align perfectly with your mission and vision.

Our structured methodology transforms the often chaotic grant process into a clear, manageable system that produces consistent results.

Our Mission

FundingForge empowers nonprofit organizations to transform their funding potential through strategic grant solutions, clear pathways to success, and expert guidance that builds lasting capacity.

Our Vision

To be the trusted partner that helps mission-driven organizations forge sustainable funding futures and maximize their community impact.

Meet Our Founder & Lead Forger

With five years of experience collaborating with nonprofits across three continents, I've helped organizations raise over $2M in funding. My nonprofit work spans diverse sectors including workforce development, developmental disabilities services, and education.

In 2024, I successfully submitted the largest amount of grant proposals in the history of the organization in a single month. By turning to AI, I discovered something transformative - these new AI tools could empower nonprofit professionals in ways I never imagined possible.

Today, I combine my nonprofit expertise with my experience as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at a tech startup and AI product strategist at a social impact management consulting firm to help other nonprofit leaders implement practical AI solutions that amplify their impact.
