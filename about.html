About FundingForge

Forging impact through strategic grant solutions

Our Story

After half a decade in the nonprofit world, I've heard the same questions echo through hundreds of grant applications: "How do I get more funding like this?", "Is this grant worth pursuing?", and "How does grant funding fit with my program?". These questions reveal a deeper challenge - the struggle to transform grant writing from a reactive task into sustainable funding.

This realization led to the creation of FundingForge. We've developed an approach that answers these fundamental questions. We forge custom solutions that align with your mission, turning grant writing from a source of stress into mission impact.

Our Mission

We transform nonprofit funding by answering the fundamental questions that keep leaders awake at night. Through strategic grant solutions, we forge clear pathways to success while building lasting organizational capacity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every nonprofit leader has clear answers to their funding questions. From this clarity we see nonprofits increasing their impact with like-minded partners.

Diego Reeb, LSW -- Lead Forger

My journey began with these very questions. I noticed that while the questions were universal, the answers needed to be uniquely crafted for each organization. This understanding became the foundation of our approach at FundingForge.

We combine nonprofit experience with a background in technology and therapy to help organizations transform their grant processes. We help nonprofits answer those crucial questions that drive mission impact.
