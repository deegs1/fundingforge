This directory is for image files.

Please add the following images:
- company-image.jpg - An image representing FundingForge (recommended size: 600x400px)
- founder-image.jpg - An image of the founder (recommended size: 600x400px)
- favicon.png - A small icon for the browser tab, ideally a simplified version of the FundingForge logo featuring the flame/ember symbol (recommended size: 32x32px)

Brand Style Guide Notes:
- The FundingForge logo features a minimalist flame or ember symbol
- The flame is designed with clean, geometric lines rather than intricate details
- Primary colors are Forge Black (#1A1A1A), Ember Red (#D64045), and Steel Gray (#505050)
- Secondary colors are Paper White (#F5F5F5) and Spark Gold (#FFC145)

You can also add a logo image if desired.
